ELAMI FOOD ENTRY #1

KODAWARI, MAKATI

Went to Kodawari in Makati and the overall rating is 8/10 - The dishes are visually appealing and well-arranged. Likely diverse and flavorful, with a good balance of rich, savory, and fresh elements. The meal offers a wide range of textures and flavors, from creamy spreads and crispy mushrooms to hearty noodles and nutritious grain bowls. The ingredients appear fresh and well-prepared, contributing to an overall high-quality dining experience. The dishes seem versatile enough to cater to different dietary preferences and tastes.

ELAMI TRAVEL ENTRY #2

RAMEN NAGI, AYALA FELIZ BRANCH

8/10 - The ramen is visually inviting with a good balance of colors and garnishes. This bowl of ramen provides a comforting and delicious meal, with a rich broth, tender pork, and a variety of fresh and flavorful toppings. It is a great representation of a well-executed ramen dish, offering both taste and visual appeal.
