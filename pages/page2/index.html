AirPods Max Features

  • Audio Quality
    • High-Fidelity Audio: Custom acoustic design with a 40mm dynamic driver provides rich, deep bass, accurate mid-range, and crisp high frequencies.
    • Active Noise Cancellation: Uses six outward-facing microphones to detect environmental noise and two inward-facing microphones to measure what you’re hearing, cancelling it with anti-noise.
    • Transparency Mode: Allows you to hear and interact with the world around you by blending in outside noise with what you’re listening to.
    • Adaptive EQ: Adjusts the sound to the fit and seal of the ear cushions to ensure immersive audio.
  • Comfort and Design
    • Knit Mesh Canopy: Distributes weight and reduces on-head pressure.
    • Memory Foam Ear Cushions: Create an effective seal for immersive sound.
    • Stainless Steel Frame: Wrapped with a soft-touch material for a combination of strength, flexibility, and comfort.
    • Telescoping Arms: Extend smoothly and stay where you set them for a consistent fit and seal.
  • User Experience
    • Digital Crown: Inspired by the Apple Watch, it offers precise volume control and the ability to play or pause audio, skip tracks, answer or end phone calls, and activate Siri.
    • Siri: Always-on voice assistant for hands-free control.
    • Spatial Audio: With dynamic head tracking to place sound all around you, creating a theater-like experience for content recorded in 5.1, 7.1, and Dolby Atmos.
    • Automatic Switching: Seamlessly move between your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
  • Battery Life and Charging
    • Battery Life: Up to 20 hours of high-fidelity audio, talk time, or movie playback with Active Noise Cancellation and spatial audio enabled.
    • Smart Case: Puts AirPods Max in an ultralow-power state that preserves battery charge when not in use.
    • Charging: Through the Lightning connector.
  • Connectivity and Additional Features
    • H1 Chips: Each ear cup has an Apple-designed H1 chip that delivers computational audio capabilities.
    • On-head Detection: Automatically pauses playback when AirPods Max are removed and resumes when you put them back on.
    • Audio Sharing: Allows you to share audio between two sets of AirPods on your Apple device.
    • Bluetooth 5.0: Ensures stable and efficient connectivity with Apple devices and other Bluetooth-enabled devices.
